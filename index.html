Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our free fonts

Are these fonts really free for commercial use?

Yes! All fonts in our collection are free for both personal and commercial use. We verify the license of each font before adding it to our collection.

How do I download and install these fonts?

Simply click the download button on any font page. After downloading the font file, you can install it on your computer by double-clicking the file and clicking "Install". For detailed instructions, check our installation guide.

Can I use these fonts on my website?

Absolutely! Most of our fonts can be used on websites. Many are available through Google Fonts or have web font versions available for download.

How often do you add new fonts?

We add new fonts to our collection weekly. We're always on the lookout for high-quality free fonts to add to our library.

Can I request a specific font?

Yes! We welcome font suggestions. If you're looking for a specific style of font, contact us and we'll try to find a high-quality free version for you.
